RepGather: Adaptive Challenge Engine Specification1. Project OverviewTransition the current static challenge logic to a Dynamic Adaptive Target system. The app will now recalculate daily goals in real-time based on the user's actual performance, using a "Spread the Debt" model to redistribute missed reps across remaining days.2. Core Logic: The "Spread the Debt" EngineInstead of a fixed daily goal, the Daily Target must be recalculated every time the user logs in or adds a rep.The Formula:$$DailyTarget = \lceil \frac{TotalGoal - CurrentTotalReps}{RemainingDays} \rceil \times ModeMultiplier$$TotalGoal: The original commitment (e.g., 1,000 push-ups).CurrentTotalReps: Total reps officially logged to date.RemainingDays: Days left in the challenge, including today.Ceiling ($\lceil \rceil$): Always round up to ensure the user never falls behind mathematically.3. Challenge Style Definitions (Multipliers)There are three distinct styles users can choose during setup. The ModeMultiplier varies based on the current day or progress:Style 1: The AscentConcept: Start easy to build the habit, finish with high intensity.Logic: Multiplier scales based on the challenge quartile.First 25% of days: 0.7x25% to 50%: 0.9x50% to 75%: 1.1xFinal 25%: 1.3xStyle 2: Weekday WarriorConcept: Heavy loading during the work week; rest on weekends.Logic: * Monday–Friday: 1.25xSaturday–Sunday: 0.375xStyle 3: WeekenderConcept: Maintain during the week; go big on your days off.Logic: * Monday–Friday: 0.75xSaturday–Sunday: 1.625x4.

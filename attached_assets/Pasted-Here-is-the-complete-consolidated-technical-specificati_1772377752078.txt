# RepGather: Adaptive Challenge Engine Specification

## 1. Project Overview

Transition the current static challenge logic to a **Dynamic Adaptive Target** system. The app will now recalculate daily goals in real-time based on the user's actual performance, using a **"Spread the Debt"** model to redistribute missed reps across remaining days.

---

## 2. Core Logic: The "Spread the Debt" Engine

Instead of a fixed daily goal, the **Daily Target** must be recalculated every time the user logs in or adds a rep.

**The Formula:**


$$DailyTarget = \lceil \frac{TotalGoal - CurrentTotalReps}{RemainingDays} \rceil \times ModeMultiplier$$

* **TotalGoal:** The original commitment (e.g., 1,000 push-ups).
* **CurrentTotalReps:** Total reps officially logged to date.
* **RemainingDays:** Days left in the challenge, including today.
* **Ceiling ($\lceil \rceil$):** Always round up to ensure the user never falls behind mathematically.

---

## 3. Challenge Style Definitions (Multipliers)

There are three distinct styles users can choose during setup. The `ModeMultiplier` varies based on the current day or progress:

### **Style 1: The Ascent**

* **Concept:** Start easy to build the habit, finish with high intensity.
* **Logic:** Multiplier scales based on the challenge quartile.
* **First 25% of days:** 0.7x
* **25% to 50%:** 0.9x
* **50% to 75%:** 1.1x
* **Final 25%:** 1.3x



### **Style 2: Weekday Warrior**

* **Concept:** Heavy loading during the work week; rest on weekends.
* **Logic:** * **Monday–Friday:** 1.25x
* **Saturday–Sunday:** 0.375x



### **Style 3: Weekender**

* **Concept:** Maintain during the week; go big on your days off.
* **Logic:** * **Monday–Friday:** 0.75x
* **Saturday–Sunday:** 1.625x
